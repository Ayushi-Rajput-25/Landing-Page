About Us

Evolvere, the departmental club of Biotechnology of National Institute of Technology, Durgapur was established in the year of 2018 with the purpose of providing a platform to people with a shared interest in Biotechnology, to nurture their ideas and focus on building networks that bridges gap between the academia and Biotechnology sectors. The club aims to develop a conducive environment in the department to promote students to develop creative thoughts to fulfil societal needs and train students as per the current demands of the industry. To achieve this aim, Evolvere organizes various events like paper presentation events, counselling sessions, publishing magazines and conduct workshops to foster the interest of students and educate them about progressions in the field Biotechnology and inspire them to contribute into the same. Evolvere is thus working to accomplish its vision of directing individual accomplishments towards organizational objectives that we believe will fuel common people to attain the uncommon results.
